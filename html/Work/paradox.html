Paradox

Paradox was a videogame hacking group that offered subscriptions across nearly every gaming platform. I joined Paradox when I was ten years old, and it was a pillar in my technological development.


My role within the Paradox team revolved around design. I created advertisements, graphical user interfaces, branding elements, and promotional videos, to name a few. Unfortunately, much of my early work within Paradox has been lost to time.
